Why SurfIt

SurfIt helps you decide when to paddle out by combining swell height & period, wind, and tides into a single, clear view. Get a quick snapshot of current conditions and a simple forecast for the next few days.

Explore an interactive map of nearby spots, save favourites, and find essentials like parking, cafes, and surf schools. Upgrade to SurfIt Pro for smart “best day” picks and richer multi‑day detail.
